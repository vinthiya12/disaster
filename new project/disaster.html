Types of Disasters

Earthquakes

Sudden shaking of the ground caused by the movement of tectonic plates.

Precaution: Drop, cover, and hold on. Secure heavy furniture. Have an emergency kit.

Floods

An overflow of water onto normally dry land.

Precaution: Move to higher ground. Do not walk or drive through floodwaters. Follow evacuation orders.

Wildfires

Uncontrolled fires that spread quickly through vegetation.

Precaution: Clear brush around your home. Have an evacuation plan. Stay informed about fire warnings.

Hurricanes

Powerful storms with strong winds and heavy rain.

Precaution: Secure your home. Stock up on supplies. Follow evacuation orders.

Tornadoes

Violently rotating columns of air that touch the ground.

Precaution: Seek shelter in a basement or interior room. Stay away from windows.

Tsunamis

Large ocean waves caused by underwater earthquakes or landslides.

Precaution: Move to higher ground immediately. Stay away from the coast.
